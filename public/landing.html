Platform Features
Built for
campus life.
Every feature designed around how students actually move, eat, and live on campus.

🛣️ Route-Based Matching

Students post their walking route and timing. Orders are matched to carriers already heading that direction — no wasted trips.

📦 Order Bundling

Multiple orders from the same food court area get bundled together. One carrier, multiple drops, maximum efficiency.

💰 Flexible Tips

You set the tip. Carriers choose orders based on value. Fair for everyone — no surge pricing, no hidden fees.

⭐ Trust Scores

Every delivery builds your reputation. Higher trust means faster matches and priority access during peak hours.

💬 In-App Chat

Real-time messaging between requester and carrier. Coordinate details, share updates, no phone number exchange needed.
